OVERVIEW OF NESSY

Nessy Foundation is a compassionate nonprofit organization based in Los Angeles, dedicated to spearheading the fight against homelessness and poverty. Founded on the principles of compassion and integrity, Nessy Foundation envisions a world where every individual has a place to call home and access to the resources needed for a dignified life.
